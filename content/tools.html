After leaving Recruit Training Command, you will need to access several Career Tool websites that you were exposed to during your training. Realizing that you are exposed to vast amounts of information we are providing the following table of tools, their associated websites and some other useful information.

Useful Websites

BUPERS Online (BOL)
Website: https://www.bol.navy.mil

Access Information: First time users can CAC logon or manual logon. Manual logon ID will be your SSN and the password will be your birthday. (YYYYMMDD) Manual logon users must change their password but the logon ID will always be your SSN. Complete the "Update Personal Information" page. Your account has been created

Help Available: 800-951-NAVY, 800-951-6289
https://www.bol.navy.mil/Help.aspx

DoD Self-Service Logon (DS Logon)
Website: https://myaccess.dmdc.osd.mil

First time users must click on "Register a DoD self-Service logon. Select authentication method you are using. (CAC) Select all applicable names you are requesting on your account. Create a password and select security questions. You will be assigned a user ID. Each individual web site visited through DS Logon will require you to create a new account at that site.

Help Available: 703-696-5790
https://myaccess.dmdc.osd.mil

Electronic Service Record (ESR)
Website: https://nsips.nmci.navy.mil

First time users must click on "ESR Self-Service (New User)" to create an account. Member must supply an official email account when requested. ESR through NSIPS does not supply you with a user name and password, this site is only available through CAC Logon.

Help Available: 877-589-5991
Email:Nsipshelpdesk@navy.mil

myPay
Website: https://mypay.dfas.mil

First time users can CAC login or click on "Create an Account". You must enter your SSN twice (as both username and password) then create your own Login ID and Password. Once completed, you may CAC log into myPay.

Help Available: 888-332-7411

Navy Knowledge Online (NKO)
Website: https://wwwa.nko.navy.mil

First time users must click on "Register as a new user". Follow the instructions on the screen to create an account. You will be assigned a login ID and be required to choose a password. The login ID will be in the format of your name. Upon completion, you may CAC log into NKO.

Help Available: 877-253-7122
Option 2, Email:netc.helpdesk@navy.mil

Professional Military Training Correspondence Courses
Website: https://www.sas.cnet.navy.mil/Login/SASLogin.aspx?redirecturl=https://www.courses.netc.navy.mil/

First time users can CAC login or use their NKO user name and ID. Follow the instructions on the screen to update your profile.
