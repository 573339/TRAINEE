After leaving Recruit Training Command, you will need to access several Career Tool websites that you were exposed to during your training. Realizing that you are exposed to vast amounts of information we are providing the following table of tools, their associated websites and some other useful information.

Useful Websites

Tool Website Access Information Help Available
BUPERS Online (BOL) https://www.bol.navy.mil First time users can CAC logon or manual logon. Manual logon ID will be your SSN and the password will be your birthday. (YYYYMMDD) Manual logon users must change their password but the logon ID will always be your SSN. Complete the "Update Personal Information" page. Your account has been created. 800-951-NAVY 800-951-6289 https://www.bol.navy.mil/Help.aspx
DoD Self-Service Logon (DS Logon) https://myaccess.dmdc.osd.mil First time users must click on "Register a DoD self-Service logon. Select authentication method you are using. (CAC) Select all applicable names you are requesting on your account. Create a password and select security questions. You will be assigned a user ID. Each individual web site visited through DS Logon will require you to create a new account at that site. 703-696-5790

Electronic Service Record (ESR) https://nsips.nmci.navy.mil First time users must click on "ESR Self-Service (New User)" to create an account. Member must supply an official email account when requested. ESR through NSIPS does not supply you with a user name and password, this site is only available through CAC Logon. 877-589-5991 e-mail: Nsipshelpdesk@navy.mil
myPay https://mypay.dfas.mil First time users can CAC login or click on "Create an Account". You must enter your SSN twice (as both username and password) then create your own Login ID and Password. Once completed, you may CAC log into myPay. 888-332-7411
Navy Knowledge Online (NKO) https://wwwa.nko.navy.mil First time users must click on "Register as a new user". Follow the instructions on the screen to create an account. You will be assigned a login ID and be required to choose a password. The login ID will be in the format of your name. Upon completion, you may CAC log into NKO. 877-253-7122 option 2 e-mail: netc.helpdesk@navy.mil
