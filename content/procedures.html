PROCEDURE

    Note 1: RDCs will utilize the sinks in the laundry room of their compartment.

    Note 2: RDC's will pour the bleach into the sinks.

    1. Fill the dual sinks half way with clean water.
    2. Add 3 caps (water bottle) of household bleach to the sinks.

      3. Note 3: Achieve at least 3 oz per gallon of water or 25ml per liter of water.


      CAUTION:Due to potential skin irritation, gloves, goggles and apron will be worn by personnel handling canteens in bleach / water solution.

    3. Ensure each water bottle has been emptied prior to submersion.
    4. Completely submerge caps and water bottle in the sink.

      5. Note 4: Ensure water bottle is filled completely with bleach / water.

    5. Allow submerged water bottles to soak in bleach / water for at least 30 minutes.
    6. Carefully empty the water bottles into the sink.
    7. Empty the sinks.

      8. Note 5: Begin rinsing water bottle immediately to avoid letting the bleach / water mixture to dry.

    8. Rinse water bottle by filling the empty water bottle Â¼ full of fresh clean water.
    9. Replace cap on water bottle.
    10. Swirl the water in the water bottle to rinse all inside surfaces.
    11. Open the water bottle and empty rinse water into sink. Rinse residue from water bottle exterior and cap.
    12. Fill water bottle with fresh water for normal use and close the cap.
    13. Empty and clean the sinks.
