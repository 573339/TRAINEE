ATTENTION!

  1. Knock three times
  2. Enter when told. Take three 30inch paces.
  3. If in a duty status, stay covered, salute.
  4. If not in a duty status, uncover and stand at attention

    5. SOUND OFF

  5. Good (Morning, Afternoon, Evening), Sir or Ma'am, or title
  6. Sr DIV# reporting Sir or Ma'am, or title.
  7. Remain at attention and state business.
  8. When dismissed, do an about face and leave office.
