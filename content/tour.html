A. Introduction

This laboratory will provide you with more intense hands-on training for mooring a ship to the pier and taking in mooring lines in preparations for getting the ship underway. The tour of the USS MARLINESPIKE will help you understand some of the shipboard conditions and familiarize you with the equipment learned from the Basic Seamanship classes.
